Buzz Wire Challenge

This is a digital version of the classic buzz wire game. The objective is to move the glowing ring from the starting area on the left side to the finishing area on the right side. You must guide the ring carefully along the path and avoid any contact with the illuminated wire. Every time the ring touches the wire the touch counter increases and a warning effect appears. The faster you complete the course with the fewest touches the better your result will be.

Press START to begin

Use smooth, controlled movements to navigate the ring along the path without brushing the wire. Practice improves both speed and accuracy over multiple tries. The design keeps everything clean and focused — no extra clutter, fully responsive on desktop and mobile, with neon colors that make the wire and ring stand out dramatically against the dark background.

  • The central game box shows a dark background with a glowing cyan wire path that curves in elegant S-shapes.
  • Green semi-transparent strip on the left = start zone with dashed border.
  • Red semi-transparent strip on the right = finish zone with dashed border.
  • Bright blue ring with cyan glow follows your mouse or finger movement.
  • Status message shows real-time time and touch count during play.
  • Two purple buttons (START and RESET) control the game.
